


### cal, ncal — displays a calendar and the date of Easter



  Basic usage of cal:

	cal

  The -y option will show all the months of the year:

	cal -y


  To view a calendar for a different year just add the year:


	cal -y 1984

  To view a specific month just add the number of the month:

	cal 2 1984
 

  Adding a -3 will show 3 months at a time:


	cal -3
