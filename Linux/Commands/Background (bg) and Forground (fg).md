





	Background (bg) and Foreground (fg)



  To send a process or action to the background press ctrl + z and can continue in 
  terminal and then press fg to bring back to the foreground.


  To send a program straight to the baclground add the & after it:

	htop &


  Typing jobs will show the  tasks running in the background:

	jobs


  To bring back a specific job type fg with the corresponding number to it:

	fg 1 (or whichever number it would be)
